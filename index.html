Frequently Asked Questions

Still have something to ask? Contact our sales team

How do I schedule an appointment?

Scheduling an appointment at Medi Bridge is easy. You can call our office directly or use our online appointment booking system on our website. Simply select your preferred date and time, and our staff will confirm your appointment promptly.

What types of healthcare services does we offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of healthcare services including primary care, specialized treatments, preventive care, and emergency services.

Are walk-in appointments available?

Yes, we do accept walk-in appointments based on availability. However, we recommend scheduling an appointment in advance to ensure you receive care at your preferred time.

Does Medi Bridge accept insurance plans?

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans. Please contact our office to verify that your specific insurance plan is accepted.

What safety measures does Medi Bridge have?

We maintain the highest safety standards including regular sanitization, safety protocols, and trained staff to ensure patient and staff safety.
